Provide a substantive interpretation of the model comparisons below.

The residual variance was not significantly reduced by the inclusion of the interaction, while AIC was reduced by roughly half a point. The AIC weights therefore indicated that both models with/without the interaction were equally probable, and the more parsimonious model is therefore preferred.

  1. Provide a plot of the interaction
library(visreg)
visreg(m3, "income", by = "age", overlay = TRUE)

Provide a substantive interpretation of the plot.

As income increases depression scores generally decrease. However, as age increases, the impact of income becomes less negative. However, because the interaction was not significant, these differences were likely due to random sampling variability, and it is likely that the effect of income does not actually depend upon the age of the individual.
